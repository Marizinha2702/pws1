World Wide Web

The WorldWideWeb (W3) is a broad-area hypermedia information retrieval initiative aiming to give universal access to a large universe of documents.
Everything online about W3 is directly or indirectly linked to this document, including an executive summary of the project, discussion lists, policy, W3 news from November, and frequently asked questions.
